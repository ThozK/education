Kanji's Level
and
Kakijun

  • This app provides searching for kanji levels and Kakijun.
  • Kakijun means the sequence for writing kanji. If you write Kanji in the correct sequence with Kakijun, it might be beautiful, and you could understand its structure easily.
  • These kanji levels indicate the grade of elementary school children's learning.
  • Enter your text, confirm the level, and click on a kanji to get its Kakijun.
